Refund Policy - IndieFilmClass

This refund policy ("Policy") governs the refund process for courses or activities offered by IndieFilmClass ("the School"). Please carefully review this Policy before enrolling in any courses or activities.

Eligibility for Refunds

a. Refunds are available for cancellations made up to 7 days before the scheduled start date of the course or activity.

b. After the 7-day period before the course start date, no refunds will be issued.

Refund Process

To request a refund, you must notify IndieFilmClass in writing via email or through the designated communication channels provided by the School. Your refund request should include your full name, course details, and a brief explanation for the cancellation.

Refund Timeframe

Refunds will be processed within 5 days of days from the date of receipt of the refund request. Please note that it may take additional time for the refunded amount to reflect in your account, depending on your payment method and banking institution.

Non-Refundable Circumstances

The following circumstances are non-refundable:

a. Failure to attend or participate in the course or activity without prior notice.

b. Expulsion or removal from the course or activity due to violation of the School's code of conduct or policies.

c. Any expenses or costs incurred by you, such as travel expenses, accommodation, or materials purchased for the course or activity.

Course Changes or Cancellations

IndieFilmClass reserves the right to make changes to the course schedule, instructors, or content. In the event of a course cancellation by IndieFilmClass, you will be entitled to a full refund of the fees paid.

Transfer of Enrollment

In certain cases, IndieFilmClass may allow you to transfer your enrollment to another course or activity. Such transfers will be subject to availability and at the discretion of IndieFilmClass.

Contact Information

For refund requests or any further inquiries, please contact IndieFilmClass through the provided communication channels, including email or the designated contact form on the School's website.

Please note that by enrolling in a course or activity offered by IndieFilmClass, you acknowledge and agree to abide by the terms and conditions outlined in this refund policy.
