#IndieFilmClasses

Learn How To Make Independent Films

At #IndieFilmClasses, we recognize the importance of empowering filmmakers to create high-quality films even with limited resources. Through our comprehensive courses, we'll teach you how to optimize your creativity, leverage cost-effective equipment and techniques, and make strategic decisions that elevate the production value of your films. Our mission is to help you realize your artistic vision and produce compelling, professional-grade films that captivate audiences, regardless of budget constraints.

About #IndieFilmClasses

Welcome to Indie Film Classes! We are dedicated to providing aspiring filmmakers with the tools and knowledge necessary to bring their unique visions to life on the big screen. With a focus on hands-on experience and individual creativity, our program empowers students to develop their own distinct styles and voices in the world of filmmaking. Whether you are an experienced filmmaker looking to hone your skills or a newcomer to the craft, we are committed to helping you achieve your goals and bring your stories to life.

Hank Orion - founder, director, producer, screenwriter

Class Descriptions

Write impactful screenplay for an independent film

In this course, you'll gain a deep understanding of what it takes to craft a compelling and impactful screenplay for an independent film. You'll learn about the unique storytelling demands and challenges of independent films, and how to use that to your advantage as you develop your script. You'll also discover tips and techniques for improving your writing and taking your screenwriting skills to new heights. I'll share my own experiences making independent films, including my mistakes and lessons learned, so you can avoid common pitfalls and bring your vision to life.

Course Fee: £250

Location: 30 Churchill Pl, London E14 5RE (5 mins away from Canary Wharf station)

Course Content

Lesson 1

Screenplay Format, Software, Title, The Whos

Lesson 2

What Kind Of Film Are You Writing

Lesson 3

Character Research

Lesson 4

How To Write A Good Backstory

Lesson 5

Beginning, Middle And An End

Lesson 6

How To Approach Rewrites
