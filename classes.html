Class Descriptions

Write impactful screenplay for an independent film

In this course, you'll gain a deep understanding of what it takes to craft a compelling and impactful screenplay for an independent film. You'll learn about the unique storytelling demands and challenges of independent films, and how to use that to your advantage as you develop your script. You'll also discover tips and techniques for improving your writing and taking your screenwriting skills to new heights. I'll share my own experiences making independent films, including my mistakes and lessons learned, so you can avoid common pitfalls and bring your vision to life.

Course Fee: £250

Location: 30 Churchill Pl, London E14 5RE (5 mins away from Canary Wharf station)

Course Content

Lesson 1

Introduction to Screenplay Writing: In the first lesson, we will dive into the world of screenplay writing. We will explore the importance of screenplay format in independent film and how it differs from other writing formats. Additionally, we will introduce you to the best screenwriting software available on the market and provide guidance on how to effectively use it. You will learn about formatting guidelines, industry standards, and best practices for laying out your screenplay.

Lesson 2

Developing a Producer's Mindset: In this lesson, we will focus on developing a producer's mindset, which is crucial for independent filmmakers. We will discuss the limitations and constraints of independent filmmaking and how to work within them. You will discover strategies for coming up with a catchy and attention-grabbing movie title that stands out. Furthermore, we will delve into the business aspects of filmmaking, preparing you to approach the writing process with a comprehensive understanding of the industry.

Lesson 3

Crafting a Compelling Story: We will guide you through the process of conducting thorough research as a writer and incorporating it into your screenplay effectively. We will explore the key story points that shape your narrative and teach you how to structure them in a way that engages the audience. Additionally, we will delve into character development, helping you create well-rounded and relatable characters that drive your story forward.

Lesson 4

Enhancing Plot and Backstory: In Lesson 4, we will focus on enhancing your plot and backstory. You will learn the importance of writing a compelling backstory that adds depth and complexity to your characters. We will explore supporting plot elements and teach you how to incorporate them seamlessly into your screenplay. Furthermore, we will discuss techniques for developing subplots that enrich your main storyline and contribute to a more dynamic narrative.

Lesson 5

Mastering the Screenplay Writing Process: This lesson is dedicated to mastering the screenplay writing process. We will provide you with invaluable tips and tricks to elevate your writing skills. We will guide you through crafting a captivating beginning that grabs the audience's attention and sets the tone for your film. Moreover, we will teach you techniques for creating a well-paced and engaging middle section that keeps the audience hooked. Finally, we will explore strategies for crafting a satisfying ending that resolves conflicts and leaves a lasting impact on the viewers.

Lesson 6

The Rewrite Process and Beyond: In the final lesson, we will tackle the rewrite process and discuss its importance in refining your screenplay. You will learn valuable techniques for identifying areas that need improvement and making effective revisions. Additionally, we will delve into the topic of copyright, helping you understand how to protect your work. Lastly, we will explore the next steps after completing your screenplay, including pitching your ideas, networking within the industry, and finding funding opportunities for your independent film projects.

Learn everything from pre-production to editing in 5 weeks

This class is specifically tailored for individuals who aspire to become filmmakers and want to learn how to create high-quality films with limited resources. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced filmmaker looking to improve your skills, this course is suitable for you. Throughout the course, you will learn the ins and outs of making a film on your own, including tips on how to make the most of different types of equipment such as cinema cameras, smartphones, and GoPros. You will also learn how to create the appearance of a higher budget film, even with limited resources.

Course Fee: £250

Location: 30 Churchill Pl, London E14 5RE (5 mins away from Canary Wharf station)

Course Content

Lesson 1

Securing A Story: In this lesson, you will discover the importance of a compelling story in independent filmmaking. You will learn techniques for developing and refining your story ideas, finding inspiration from various sources, and crafting engaging characters and plots. We will explore ways to write a screenplay on a low/no budget and discuss the key elements of a strong narrative.

Lesson 2

Where To Find Money: This lesson focuses on the various funding options available for independent films. We will delve into strategies for fundraising and securing financing on a limited budget. You will explore crowdfunding platforms and gain insights into running successful campaigns. Additionally, we will explore avenues such as grants, sponsorships, and partnerships, as well as alternative sources of funding and resources.

Lesson 3

Hiring The Right Crew: In Lesson 3, you will learn about the crucial roles and responsibilities within a film crew. We will discuss the importance of building a team of dedicated and passionate individuals and explore effective networking strategies for finding collaborators within your budget constraints. You will gain insights into negotiating contracts and agreements with crew members and learn how to manage and motivate your team throughout the production process.

Lesson 4

Production: This lesson will cover the planning and organization of your film production. You will learn how to create a realistic shooting schedule and budget, maximize resources, and utilize available locations effectively. We will discuss strategies for achieving effective production design on a low budget and explore ways to overcome common production challenges while keeping everything under control.

Lesson 5

Post Production: In Lesson 5, we will dive into the post-production process and its significance in creating a high-quality film. You will gain an introduction to editing software and learn the basics of efficient editing, color grading, and sound design. We will also explore techniques for adding visual effects and enhancing the overall production value. Lastly, you will discover how to prepare your film for distribution and festival submissions.

Lesson 6

Making Your Money Back: The final lesson will focus on marketing and monetizing your independent film. You will learn strategies for promoting your film, identifying your target audience, and creating a comprehensive marketing plan. We will explore the utilization of social media and online platforms for distribution, as well as the potential of film festivals, screenings, and other distribution channels. Lastly, we will discuss monetization options and ways to recoup your investment through sales and licensing.
