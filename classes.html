Screenwriting For Indie Film

Coming Soon

In this class, you'll gain a deep understanding of what it takes to craft a compelling and impactful screenplay for an independent film. You'll learn about the unique storytelling demands and challenges of independent films, and how to use that to your advantage as you develop your script. You'll also discover tips and techniques for improving your writing and taking your screenwriting skills to new heights. I'll share my own experiences making independent films, including my mistakes and lessons learned, so you can avoid common pitfalls and bring your vision to life.

Course Content:

Lesson 1

Introduction to Screenplay Writing and Formatting

Lesson 2

Developing a Producer's Mindset and Understanding the Industry

Lesson 3

Crafting a Compelling Story and Character Development

Lesson 4

Enhancing Plot, Backstory, and Subplots

Lesson 5

Mastering the Screenplay Writing Process and Crafting a Memorable Ending

Learn How To Make Real-Time Feature Films

Coming Soon

This intensive class is tailored for individuals who possess a solid understanding of the filmmaking process and techniques, aiming to elevate their skills and master the art of creating visually stunning real-time films in just few days. Whether driven by budget constraints or personal aspirations. Throughout the course, you will embark on a comprehensive learning journey, spanning from crafting captivating narratives suitable for real-time film to mastering camera operation on a shoot day, all the way to proficiently capturing footage and honing your editing skills.

Course Content:

Lesson 1

Writing a great story for real-time film

Lesson 2

Essential Pre-production before filming

Lesson 3

Hiring crew and casting actors

Lesson 4

Choosing the right equipment for your film

Lesson 5

Filming techniques and working with limited time

Lesson 6

Editing and Distribution

* Class Requirements: Basic understanding of camera techniques, screenwriting, and the production process.
