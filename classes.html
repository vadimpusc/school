Class Descriptions

Write impactful screenplay for an independent film

In this course, you'll gain a deep understanding of what it takes to craft a compelling and impactful screenplay for an independent film. You'll learn about the unique storytelling demands and challenges of independent films, and how to use that to your advantage as you develop your script. You'll also discover tips and techniques for improving your writing and taking your screenwriting skills to new heights. I'll share my own experiences making independent films, including my mistakes and lessons learned, so you can avoid common pitfalls and bring your vision to life.

Course Fee: £250

Location: 30 Churchill Pl, London E14 5RE (5 mins away from Canary Wharf station)

Course Content

Lesson 1

Introduction to Screenplay Writing and Formatting: Learn the fundamentals of screenplay writing, including format and structure for independent films. Discover the best screenwriting software and gain practical knowledge on how to effectively use it.

Lesson 2

Developing a Producer's Mindset and Understanding the Industry: Develop a producer's mindset to navigate the constraints of independent filmmaking. Explore strategies for creating attention-grabbing movie titles and gain insights into the business aspects of the industry.

Lesson 3

Crafting a Compelling Story and Character Development: Learn how to conduct research effectively and incorporate it into your screenplay. Explore key story points and structure your narrative to engage the audience. Develop well-rounded and relatable characters.

Lesson 4

Enhancing Plot, Backstory, and Subplots: Enhance your plot by creating a compelling backstory that adds depth to your characters. Discover techniques for incorporating supporting plot elements and developing enriching subplots.

Lesson 5

Mastering the Screenplay Writing Process and Crafting a Memorable Ending: Master the screenplay writing process with valuable tips and tricks. Craft a captivating beginning, a well-paced middle section, and a satisfying ending that leaves a lasting impact.

Learn everything from pre-production to editing in 5 weeks

This class is specifically tailored for individuals who aspire to become filmmakers and want to learn how to create high-quality films with limited resources. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced filmmaker looking to improve your skills, this course is suitable for you. Throughout the course, you will learn the ins and outs of making a film on your own, including tips on how to make the most of different types of equipment such as cinema cameras, smartphones, and GoPros. You will also learn how to create the appearance of a higher budget film, even with limited resources.

Course Fee: £350

Location: 30 Churchill Pl, London E14 5RE (5 mins away from Canary Wharf station)

Course Content

Lesson 1

Crafting Compelling Stories: Learn the essentials of developing captivating stories for independent filmmaking. Discover techniques for refining ideas, creating engaging characters and plots, and writing a screenplay on a low/no budget. Understand the key elements of a strong narrative.

Lesson 2

Fundraising and Financing: Explore various funding options for independent films on a limited budget. Gain insights into successful crowdfunding campaigns and discover grants, sponsorships, and partnerships. Uncover alternative sources of funding and resources.

Lesson 3

Casting and Building the Right Team: Understand the crucial roles within a film crew and learn effective networking strategies for finding collaborators within your budget constraints. Gain insights into negotiating contracts and agreements, and master the art of managing and motivating your team.

Lesson 4

Efficient Production: Learn how to plan and organize your film production effectively. Create a realistic shooting schedule and budget, optimize resources, and make the most of available locations. Overcome production challenges while maintaining control.

Lesson 5

Enhancing Film Quality: Delve into the post-production process and its significance in creating a high-quality film. Explore efficient editing techniques, color grading, sound design, and adding visual effects. Prepare your film for distribution and festival submissions.

* Class Requirements: Basic understanding of camera techniques, screenwriting, and the production process.
